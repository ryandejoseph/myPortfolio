Back then, I was just this kid who likes to play online games. May it be on mobile phone or on a computer, it doesn't matter as long as I enjoy playing. When I got to highschool, my interest in gaming continues to grow. I just found myself going to a computer shop every weekend to play with my friends. I can still recall one of our favorite game which is DOTA. The moment that the game would start, the thrill and excitement inside me is overflowing. I would say that, this was my temporary way of escaping reality. This is also the time when my curiosity kicks in, thinking how these games are created. That's the time when I found out that a game is made up of thousand of lines of code for it to be able to function. This was the very moment I discovered programming. Which leads me to develop my deep interest in technology and the reason why I chosed Information Technology as my college program. Hoping that one day I will be able to design and develop websites, systems or applications that would bring solutions, convenience and even entertainment to different people from day to day lives.

Technology brings convenience to our lives and made our daily tasks easier. And as a programmer, it is my dream to develop web and mobile applications with the aim of helping people in their day-to-day lives. My primary goal for now is to become a full-stack developer. As of the moment, I'm already indulging myself in improving my skills and knowledge both in front-end and back-end field. It may not be easy, but I know in myself that I'm ready to take this challenge. If ever time would allow me to explore more, I would also love to see myself in the world of game development. However, I believe that before I can fully achieve these things, I need to have a real work experience in a tech industry. To which, I'm close to finding one.
